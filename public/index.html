About Loren

Loren is a creative technology professional with 20 years of industry experience. As a leader, a manager, a developer and a thinker, Loren has made tech organizations more effective while leveraging technology for competitive advantage.

Axilent

Founded by Loren in 2011, Axilent is the maker of ACE, the Adaptive Context Engine. Capable of domain specific product and content recommendations, and behavioral user profiling, ACE enables websites and applications to dynamically respond to the context of individual users. ACE has applications in e-commerce, publishing and matchmaking (such as dating, job search etc.).

Using ACE as a platform, Axilent has empowered clients in the e-commerce, publishing and startup spaces.

Conversational Architecture and CAVE Language

Noticing the lack of a common language for describing contextual applications (such as those that are enabled by ACE), Loren developed a means of expressing such applications based on the metaphor of a natural language conversation, called Conversational Architecture.

Building on this approach, Loren developed a whiteboard-friendly visual design language for articulating contextual app behavior using the conversational metaphor. Called CAVE Language (Conversational Architecture Visual Expression), it allows application designers from different disciplinary backgrounds (Business, UX, Tech etc.) to discuss contextual applications in a common language.

Loren recently presented CAVE Language at World IA Day in New York City. The video of his talk is on YouTube, and the slides can be found on SlideShare.
